Departments

We have the best well equipied Department to serve the students for excellent studing

Computer Science And Engineering

Department of Computer Science and Engineering department was established in the year 1997 since the inception of the institute. The department offers, BE in Computer Science and Engineering, MSc Engineering by Research and Ph.D programs in Computer Science and Engineering. The total intake for the BE program is 90. The department has state of art laboratories and well qualified faculty members. The graduated students from the department are working in reputed organizations across the Globe.

Information Science And Engineering

Vivekananda Institute of technology pioneers the Department of Information Science and Engineering in the year 1998 with the sanctioned intake of 60. The department has well equipped laboratories and highly qualified dynamic faculty members. The department aims to achieve quality in education and excellence in knowledge management through innovations, problem solving, inter disciplinary collaborations and continuous improvement to meet the changing needs of society.

Electronics and Communication

The Department of Electronics and Communication was established in the year 1997 with an intake of 60 and the present intake is 120. The department is equipped with excellent infrastructure with state-of-art laboratories, equipment, software, latest computers and e-classroom with smart boards.

Civil Engineering

The Department of Civil Engineering has been in existence at VKIT since 2013 with an intake of 60. The faculty with highest qualification and experience trains the students in the domines of Planning, Design, Construction and Management etc.

Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical Engineering, mother of engineering contributes enormously for the growth of technology over the centuries has been in existence since 2013 with an intake of 60. The areas of study includes Manufacturing processes, Mechanical vibrations, Mechatronics, Heat and Mass Transfer, Tool Engineering and Design, Design of machine elements, Computer Integrated Manufacturing and Robotics.

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

The Department of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning is established in the year 2021, offers an undergraduate programme BE under the affiliation of Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belgaum. VKIT thrives to impart an effective technical education by means of experiential learning and thereby develop positive learning experiences.
